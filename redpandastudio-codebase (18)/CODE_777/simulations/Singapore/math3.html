Step 5: "Regrouping" Explained

In Singapore Math, we don't just "carry". We Regroup.

When a column adds up to 10 or more, we exchange 10 smaller units for 1 bigger unit.

Step 7: The Subtraction Setup

Now, let's look at subtraction: 8,304,612 − 2,578,945.

Often, the top digit is smaller than the bottom digit. We cannot just flip them!

Warning: 2 - 5 is NOT 3.

Step 8: "Borrowing" is "Exchanging"

Instead of "borrowing" (and never giving back), we Exchange.

If we need more Ones, we take 1 Ten and break it into 10 Ones.

1 Ten ➡ 10 Ones

Step 10: The Estimation Check

Before you hand in your work, Estimate. It catches big mistakes.

Problem: 8,304,612 − 2,578,945

Round to nearest Million:

8,304,612 ≈ 8,000,000
2,578,945 ≈ 3,000,000
Estimated Result: 5,000,000

Actual Answer: 5,725,667. Is it reasonable? Yes.
