Step 9: Why Estimate?

Estimation is not just guessing. It is a tool to check reasonableness.

🧠 Mental Check

If you calculate 400 + 300 and get 7,000, estimation tells you immediately that you made a mistake.

Singapore Math teaches us to estimate before we calculate or after to verify.

Step 12: Front-End Estimation

Singapore Math uses a technique called "Front-End Estimation" for quick checks. You simply look at the leading (front) digits and ignore the rest.

6,483 + 2,514

Look at the thousands only:

6,000 + 2,000 = 8,000

This gives a lower-bound estimate quickly without complex rounding rules.

Step 14: Common Mistakes

Watch out for these traps!

❌ The Wrong Neighbor

Looking at the digit ITSELF instead of the digit to the RIGHT.

❌ Chain Rounding

Rounding the ones, then tens, then hundreds. No! Only look at the immediate neighbor.
